This is Ivey Fintech.

This year, we’re thrilled to bring you an exciting lineup of events designed to ignite your passion for fintech and kickstart your Ivey career. Get ready to explore the dynamic world of finance and technology with exclusive firm trips to gain insider insights from industry leaders, case competitions that tackle real-world situations, and so much more! Our events team has meticulously planned a series of impactful events just for you—so join us at Ivey Fintech and make the most of these incredible opportunities!

· · ·

This past semester, students from across Western and Ivey had the privilege of visiting EQ Bank and the Scotiabank Digital Factory in Toronto to explore innovative new approaches to tackling banking in Canada. From witnessing EQ Bank’s innovative banking solutions to touring the Digital Factory, every moment was filled with learning and new insights. We also had the chance to meet with industry leaders who shared their perspectives on the industry and offered valuable career advice. Thank you to EQ Bank and the Scotiabank Digital Factory for their warm hospitality and enriching experience as well as our wonderful panelists for igniting engaging discussions.

· · ·

Last year, 30 students were involved in Ivey Fintech’s Stock Simulation Competition: an event that offered students the chance to sharpen their trading skills using MarketWatch's stock simulator. Students were given a $100,000 simulated portfolio and four weeks to trade with only a single requirement: at least 50% of the portfolio needed to be invested in Fintech stocks. The teams with the top 3 highest returns will receive Amazon gift cards and mentorship opportunities. The Stock Simulation also featured a presentation by Aaru Chandra, incoming Investment Banking Summer Analyst at Evercore. Aaru walked students through the basics of investing, how to find ideal fintech investments, and shared an overview of his award-winning fintech stock pitch. We are looking forward to seeing who wins the coming stock-simulation competition and what fintech stocks shine throughout this period.
